Current day chronology of Rocinante "Buck" Cervantes

  • August 2012 - Born in a sleepy suburb of Merced, California
  • Late July 2013 - Rocinante Escaped the Merced to roam the countryside with a friendly furry accomplice, whose new home reasons that remain a mystery to Rocinante to this very day
  • Early August 2013 - Found surveying private lands deep into their journey, the canid duo are captured and turned in to the Merced Animal Care and Control
  • Mid August 2013 - After the traveling pair stay unclaimed at the shelter, the pair are sadly separated never to hear about the other again. Rocinante, the more social pup of the duo, is transfered to the San Francisco SPCA: a private, no-kill Bay Area shelter in heart of the city by the bay
  • Mid August 2013 - Buck is rescued and adopted into his forever home by an SF SPCA volunteer
  • February 2015 - Buck and his humans flee to the East Bay to escape the increasing gentechtrification of San Francisco
  • July 2016 - Buck gets his wish for his own Rocinante beastie bestie. Enter "Ragnar Snowshoe Padwan Lodbrok": from the feline tribe of the same SF SPCA. After a brief uncertainty and initial mayhem at the blended household, Buck and Ragnar became fast friends.
  • September 2016 - The adventure continues...

Rocinante "Buck" Cervantes, travels, plays and loves hard with his devoted tribe and forever homies in the cities and burbs of Cali.
